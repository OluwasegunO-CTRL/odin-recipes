Amala

Description

Amala is a traditional African dish that is common among the Yoruba people of Nigeria. it is served with different soup but the common and popliar soup served is ewedu, gbegiri and pepper soup(as seen in the image above).

Amala served with ewedu, gbegiri and pepper soup is called Ablia Serve Amala with a fish or meat of your choice

Ingredients

  • Yam flour
  • Water
  • beans
  • pepper
  • Tomato
  • ewedu leave (jute)
