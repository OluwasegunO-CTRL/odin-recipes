JOLLOF RICE

Description

Jollof Rice is a delicacy common in West Africa

Jollof Rice is the combination of Rice with pepper, tomatoes and seasonings, it is mainly eaten along side Chicken or meat

There is actually a Jollof Rice war between Nigeria and Ghana about whose Jollof rice tastes better.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Pepper
  • Chicken / beef Stock
  • Seasonings
  • Groundnut/ soya beans oil

Steps

  • Watch Youtube
  • Serve hot
  • Eat with any drink of your choice
