Iyan (Pounded Yam)

Description

Iyan, also known as pounded yam is a traditional Yoruba food common among the indegines of Ondo and Ekiti State also maong the the omu aran people of Kwara.

Iyan, as the english translation says; Pounded yam. Is gotten from a well pounded yam with a Pestle and mortar.

In addition, Iyan is best served with egusi, or efo riro or any other soup you wish to eat it with.
